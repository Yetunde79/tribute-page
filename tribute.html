The first Black US President: Barack Obama

About Obama

  • He was born in Hawaii
  • His dad is from Kenya and his mom is from Kansas
  • His parents divorced in March 1964
  • He graduated from Colombia University with a BA from political science
  • He is a protestant christain
  • He started Harvard Law School in the fall of 1988 and was selected as and editor of the Harvard LAw Review in his first year.
  • He became the first black president of the Harvard Law Review in his second year
  • He worked for two years as a visiting Law and Government fellow at the University of Chicago Law School
  • He joined Davis, Miner, Barnhill & Galland Law school in 1993 and worked with them till 2004.
  • He was elected to the Illinois Senate in 1996 and reelected again in 1998 and 2000
  • He ran for Senate in 2004 and won with 70% of the votes which made him a rising star in the Democratic party
  • He resigned his Senate seat in 2008 to focus on his presidency run
  • He won the 2008 presidential election by 52.9% of popular vote
  • He ran for president again in 2012 and won by 51.1% of popular vote
